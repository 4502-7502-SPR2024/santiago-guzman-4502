7 DJ producers that everyone should listen to at least once.

  • Daft Punk

    Formed in 1993, Daft Punk is one of the most iconic and influential electronic music duos in history. Comprising Thomas Bangalter and Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo, the French pair revolutionized the genre with their innovative blend of house, funk, and rock elements. Their albums, including "Discovery" and "Random Access Memories," are regarded as classics. Daft Punk announced their retirement in 2021, leaving behind a legacy that has shaped the electronic music landscape for decades.

  • Black Coffee

    Nkosinathi Innocent Maphumulo, known as Black Coffee, is a South African DJ and producer born on March 11, 1976, in Durban. He has become a global ambassador for house music, blending African rhythms with international electronic influences. Black Coffee's rise to prominence includes numerous awards, high-profile collaborations, and a residency at Ibiza's prestigious Hi Ibiza club. His work transcends borders, showcasing the rich and diverse sound of South African house music on the world stage.
